# Fossil Group (fossil-group)

Fossil Group is a global designer, marketer, and distributor of consumer fashion accessories including watches, jewelry, handbags, small leather goods, belts, and sunglasses sold under owned and licensed brands such as Fossil, Skagen, Michele, Relic, Misfit, and licensed lines for Michael Kors, Diesel, Emporio Armani, Tory Burch, and others.
